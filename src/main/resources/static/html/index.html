ABOUT PVET

PVET Stores is a unified platform developed to simplify the sharing and management of materials across campuses. It helps ensure that resources are requested, tracked, and distributed efficiently within the institution. The portal supports three types of logins—User, Admin, and Authenticator—each with defined responsibilities. Items are clearly grouped into Academic, Maintenance, and Plastic categories to help users find what they need quickly. With built-in search tools and real-time inventory updates, PVET Stores supports better coordination, transparency, and resource planning across the campus.
